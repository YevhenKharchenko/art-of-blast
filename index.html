About the Game

Welcome to Art of Blast: Puzzle & Friends, a delightful journey through a world of colors and creativity! Match colorful blocks, solve lively puzzles, and join adorable characters on a mission to bring life and art back to the world. Relax, strategize, and blast your way to victory!

Game Features

  • Vibrant Puzzle Design

    Every match fills the world with color and life.

  • Creative Levels

    Unique challenges with plenty of twists and surprises.

  • Cute Companions

    Friendly characters join you on your artistic quest.

  • Artful Progression

    Unlock new areas and watch them bloom with creativity.

  • Powerful Boosters

    Blast through tricky levels with dazzling special moves.

Our Uniqueness

  • A combination of classic match puzzles and an evolving artistic world.

  • Heartwarming storylines that grow as you progress.

  • Charming animations that make each explosion satisfying.

  • Regular seasonal events and special in-game surprises.

  • A perfect mix of strategy and relaxation.
